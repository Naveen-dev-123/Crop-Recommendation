CUMBU - Disease Details

Disease Name

Disease Image

Medicine Details

Pearl Millet Smut:

Spray COC and Carbendazim.

Downy Mildew: Sclerospora graminicola

Grow downy mildew resistant varieties CO7, WCC 75, CO(Cu)9, TNAU-Cumbu Hybrid-CO9. Transplanting reduces disease incidence. At the time of planting infected seedlings should be removed. In the direct sown crop, infested plants should be removed up to 45 days of sowing as and when the symptoms are noticed. Spray any one of the fungicides Metalaxyl + Mancozeb @500 g or Mancozeb 1000g/ha.
