Sheath Blight: Rhizoctonia solani

Carbendazim 50 WP @ 500g/ha Azoxystrobin @ 500ml/ha Hexaconazole 75% WG @ 100mg/ lit 1st spray at the time of disease appearance and 2nd spray 15 days later.

False Smut: Ustilaginoidea virens

Two sprayings of Propiconazole 25 EC @ 500ml/ha (or) Copper hydroxide 77 WP @ 1.25 kg/ha at boot leaf and 50% flowering stages.
